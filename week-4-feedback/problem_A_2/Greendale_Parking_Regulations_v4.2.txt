SECTION 4: PERMITS AND IDENTIFICATION
4.1. Issuance. Parking permits are issued solely by the Department of Transportation.
4.2. Display. Physical hang-tags must be displayed on the rearview mirror at all times.
4.3. Privacy (Faculty Union Agreement Clause 12b). To protect faculty privacy,
automated tracking of faculty vehicle movement is prohibited. License plate data
for faculty members may only be stored for 24 hours and cannot be permanently
linked to their employee personnel file.
4.4. Transferability. Student "Commuter" passes are linked to a single vehicle.
Faculty "Gold" passes are transferable between up to three (3) registered vehicles
owned by the faculty member, provided only one vehicle is on campus at a time.

SECTION 7: VIOLATIONS AND FINES
7.1. Grace Period. A mandatory grace period of twenty (20) minutes is granted
upon entry to any lot for drop-offs. No fees shall be assessed if the vehicle
exits within this window.
7.2. Immobilization. Vehicles with three (3) or more unpaid citations are subject
to immobilization (booting).
7.3. Reserved Spaces. Usage of "Dean's List" or "Faculty" designated spots is
enforced 24/7. However, per the 2018 Accessibility Addendum, any vehicle displaying
a state-issued Handicap Placard is exempt from "Reserved" restrictions and may
park in any available non-fire-lane spot free of charge.

SECTION 9: SYSTEM RELIABILITY AND OVERRIDES
9.1. In the event of mechanical failure of access control devices, the default
state must preserve campus security. All barriers must lock in the "Down" position
until manual intervention by Campus Police.
